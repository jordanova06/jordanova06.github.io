About me

I really like Oscar Wilde's quote: To define is to limit, because it holds some truth to it but also because it gives me a reason why I don't like defining myself in a short paragraph. I'll give it a shot anyway.

My name is Angela Jordanova, I'm a 22 year old living in Skopje, Macedonia and I just graduated at the SEDC Academy for Web Design. As a person, I dislike being stagnant in all areas of my life, so I try my best to improve and grow my skillset and actively look for opportunities to expand my knowledge.

I've always had an inclination for visual arts so I focus my creative energy on drawing, photography, graphic and web design. I attended college for computer science and engineering for a couple of years until I decided that I wanted to completely focus on web design. Although I enjoyed programming, it didn't give me a chance to utilize my creativity, so I decided on web design as the middle ground. I like creating things that tell a story and offer a unique experience, so I am eager to work on similar projects with like-minded individuals.
