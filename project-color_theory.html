Color Theory

Colors are an important part of our lives, but they have even more significance in a designerâ€™s life and there are certain rules that should be followed to make a product that is visually appealing. So, this project is me implementing the rules that I learned about while also giving a brief introduction to what color theory is.

I wanted the main focus of the project to be colors, the way you can combine them to create a visual harmony, what they represent and what kind of emotions they evoke. Another idea that I wanted to implement is to make it interactive by letting the user pick a color of their choice and learn a bit more about its meaning.
